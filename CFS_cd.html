    IRA-Certificates of deposit offer the security and predictability of a Certificate of deposit plus the tax benefits of an
    Individual Retirement Account (IRA). With a CFS Traditional IRA-Certificates of deposit, your contributions may be tax-
    deductible in the tax year for which they were made, and earnings are tax-deferred until they are withdrawn. You are
    eligible to establish a traditional IRA if you're under the age of 70½ for the entire year and have earned income.
    IRA - certificate of deposit are backed by federal deposit insurance up to $250,000.

    • Available maturities: 30 days, 90 days, 182 days, one year, 18 months, two years, three years 5
    • Competitive, tiered high interest rate 2
    • Automatic renewal at maturity, with 10-day grace period after maturity to avoid early withdrawal penalty 3
    • Start making IRS penalty-free withdrawals at age 59½. 6
    • FDIC insured up to specified limits

    1 Requires $1,000 minimum opening deposit
    2 High interest paid monthly on balances of $10,000 or more, quarterly on balances below $10,000
    3 Early withdrawal penalty equal to one month rate or three months rate interest, depending on term of certificate of
    deposit
    5 For IRA - certificate of deposit with maturities of less than 90 days, and for initial deposits of less than $1,000,
    a $25 setup fee will apply.
    6 Any earnings and deductible contributions taxed as ordinary income at withdrawal. Withdrawals made prior to age 59½
    may be subject to a 10% tax penalty. Please check with your tax advisor for the proper handling of any taxable event.
